# Greetings and Farewells

|Word|Romaji|Translation|Additional Notes|
|:-----:|:------:|:-------:|:-------:|
|こんいちは|Konichiwa|Hello|An informal greeting used between 10 A.M. and sundown.|
|さようなら|Sayōnara|Goodbye|An informal farewell.|
|おはようございます|Ohayō Gozaimasu|Good morning|A formal greeting used until 10 A.M.|
|こんばんは|Konbanwa|Good evening|A formal greeting.|
|おやすみなさい|Oyasumi Nasai|Good night|Used before going to bed.|
|ではまた / じゃまた|Dewa Mata/ Ja Mata| With that/Well then|An informal farewell used when parting from friends or relatives.|
|いってらっしゃい|Itte Rasshai|So long (Person leaving)|Said to members of a household as they leave the house.|
|いってまいります|Itte Mairimasu|So long (Response)|Used in reply to いってらっしゃい.|
|ただいま|Tadaima|I'm back|Said by a person returning home.|
|おかえりなさい|Okaeri Nasai|Welcome home|Used in reply to ただいま.|
